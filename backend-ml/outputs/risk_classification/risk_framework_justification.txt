Risk Stratification Framework
========================================

Method: Percentile-based (Q1/Q3) thresholds
Q1 (25th percentile): 0.3461
Q3 (75th percentile): 0.9441

Low Risk     (Bottom 25%):  probability < 0.3461
  → Indicates general awareness level

Moderate Risk (Middle 50%): 0.3461 ≤ probability ≤ 0.9441
  → Suggests monitoring and supportive interventions

High Risk    (Top 25%):     probability > 0.9441
  → Requires priority attention and further evaluation

Justification:
Percentile-based thresholds are used to provide relative risk stratification without relying on arbitrary probability cutoffs, ensuring consistency across varying data distributions.
